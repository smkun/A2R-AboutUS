Meet Our Amazing Team

The creative hearts behind Affairs to Remember Florist

Lori Kunian

Owner & Master Florist

Lori has over 40 years of experience and is the only AIFD-trained florist in the area. She leads Affairs to Remember Florist with unmatched skill.

Her arrangements blend precision and creativity. Every design is personal, thoughtful, and full of heart. She stays serious when needed but lights up when she designs.
Lives on a beer budget with champagne taste. Her favorite flower is Stock for its soft texture and nutmeg scent.

Ben Smith

Manager

Ben is the youngest team member and a natural designer with a sharp eye. Taught by Lori, he brings energy and new ideas to every arrangement.

He experiments with bold colors, grows his own flowers, and travels to flower farms worldwide.
His eclectic music taste and love for all blooms brighten the shop daily. Customers feel welcome around his calm, kind presence.

Deb McCabe

Florist

Deb has worked at the shop for 9 years. She grew from carefree to confident and now leads with her creativity and warmth.

She studied music, plays the cello, and often sings in the shop. Her energy lifts the team and her arrangements make customers smile.
Spends time outdoors with her son, Colin. Her designs and personality bring joy and inclusion to every visitor.

Joe Spinale

Delivery Driver

Joe supports both shop and road operations. He keeps things organized and running well.

Before joining, he was a district manager at 99 Restaurant. His professionalism and management skills now strengthen the team.
Off duty, he golfs, tends his yard, and relaxes with his grandkids. He sees beauty in every bloom and brings order wherever he goes.

Sonny Candelieri

Delivery Driver

Sonny mixes humor, care, and strength. A former wrestler and a painter, he brings energy and creativity to every delivery.

He's quick with a joke and sharp with details. He makes each bouquet's journey personal.
Family comes first for Sonny. He enjoys good food and great company. His presence is warm, neat, and always remembered.
